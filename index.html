About Us

Learn a little about us...

Paradise Refined is a progressive metal band based in the Knoxville, tennessee area. Paradise started as a studio project by Ragan and Charlie (guitar/bass) inside Raganâ€™s apartment back in 2018. They wrote and released a few songs together until deciding on a hiatus. The flame was rekindled after a few years, so Ragan and Charlie decided to form it into a full band. Ragan knew Ryan (drums) from previous projects in the past and decided he would be a good fit to be involved again. Ragan also knew Preston and Skylar (vocals) from years of being acquaintances. Ragan and Charlie decided to ask Preston to join, and the rest was history. We found Seth Engle (guitar) through Ryan, and hung out with him a few times and decided he would be an excellent fit. Together over the last year, we have grown to have 1500 followers, completed the writing an album, opened for born of Osiris on our first show, and had a very successful 2nd show. There is so much more to come, and we are very thankful to have you along for the ride.
